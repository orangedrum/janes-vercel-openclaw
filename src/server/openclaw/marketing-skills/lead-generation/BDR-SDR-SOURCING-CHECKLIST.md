# BDR/SDR Sourcing Checklist

One-page reference for finding and validating BDR and SDR contacts for outreach.

## Role Definition

- **BDR (Business Development Representative)** — outbound prospecting, cold hunting, new pipeline generation
- **SDR (Sales Development Representative)** — inbound qualification, lead routing, sales support
- **Primary focus:** BDR
- **Secondary focus:** SDR

---

## Search Criteria

- [ ] Title: BDR, Business Development Representative, SDR, Sales Development Representative
- [ ] Secondary titles: Sales Development Manager, Lead Development Representative, Outbound Sales Rep
- [ ] Company size: matches your ICP (typically 50-500 employees)
- [ ] Industries: aligned with your target verticals
- [ ] Signals: hiring for sales roles, sales team expansion, go-to-market growth investments

---

## Sourcing Rules

### Source Priority

- [ ] Primary: Free public sources (LinkedIn, company websites, news, job boards)
- [ ] Secondary: Apollo free plan (supplement only, not sole source)
- [ ] Fallback: Google search + public web signals
- [ ] Never: Paid data vendors, unless explicitly approved

### Search Depth

- [ ] Minimum 15 search result pages per city or market area
- [ ] Cross-reference multiple sources before confirming a lead
- [ ] Document the source URL for each contact

---

## Email Validation Rules

### Contact Format

- [ ] Individual human email only: `firstname@domain.com` or `firstname.lastname@company.com`
- [ ] Professional company domain (not Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail, etc.)
- [ ] Exact spelling verified against public source

### Invalid Addresses (Discard)

- [ ] Generic aliases: `info@`, `sales@`, `hello@`, `contact@`, `support@`
- [ ] Role-based email: `bdr@`, `sdr@`, `outreach@`
- [ ] Any address you cannot verify with a public source

### Gmail Hover-Ready

- [ ] Email should be verifiable in Gmail (company domain, professional format)
- [ ] Contact is findable on LinkedIn or company website

---

## Deduplication

- [ ] Cross-reference against existing CRM before adding
- [ ] Cross-reference against current batch to ensure zero repeats
- [ ] Check variant spellings: John vs Jon, Robert vs Bob, etc.
- [ ] Check variant domains if company has multiple email domains

---

## Batch Quality

### Before Export

- [ ] Total leads: 100 (or target count)
- [ ] All emails validated against rules above
- [ ] All entries deduplicated
- [ ] No generic addresses in final batch
- [ ] AI personalization hook includes one market signal per contact

### Red Flags (Do Not Include)

- [ ] Email format unclear or non-standard
- [ ] Cannot verify contact via public source
- [ ] Generic or role-based email address
- [ ] Duplicate of existing CRM entry
- [ ] Contact is competitor employee (unless intentional)

---

## Hook Restrictions

- [ ] Do NOT use the word `design` in any hook
- [ ] Do NOT use the word `creative` in any hook
- [ ] Do use specific company signals: hiring, funding, product launch, tech stack change
- [ ] Do reference their role and one relevant pain point or opportunity

---

## Output Format

| Column | Rule |
|--------|------|
| Company Name | Exact legal name |
| City | City where contact is based |
| website URL | `https://company.com` format |
| email address | Verified, professional, individual |
| Target Name | First and last name |
| quality | HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW |
| AI personalization | One market signal with one sentence hook |
| Status | "Unsent" for new batches |
| Template_ID | BATCH-001-RUN-1 format |
| Touch # | 1 for first send |

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- [ ] Using generic aliases (info@, sales@, etc.)
- [ ] Including Gmail or free email addresses
- [ ] Copying contacts without deduping
- [ ] Using vague hooks without market signals
- [ ] Skipping source verification
- [ ] Including `design` or `creative` in hooks
- [ ] Sourcing from paid lists without explicit approval

---

## Approval Gate

Before sending any batch:

- [ ] All 100 emails follow contact format rules ✅
- [ ] Zero generic addresses ✅
- [ ] Zero duplicates against existing CRM ✅
- [ ] Every contact deduplicated within batch ✅
- [ ] Every personalization hook includes one market signal ✅
- [ ] No `design` or `creative` language in hooks ✅
- [ ] All sources documented for reference ✅
